# 🏪 Retail Sales Performance Dashboard

---

## Project Overview
The **Retail Sales Performance Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI project designed to provide actionable insights into sales, revenue, and customer behavior for a retail business.  

This dashboard transforms raw sales data into a **visual storytelling tool**, enabling decision-makers to quickly identify top-performing products, key revenue channels, and opportunities for growth.  

By focusing on both **trends over time** and **customer segmentation**, this project helps businesses optimize inventory, marketing, and sales strategies effectively.

## Dashboard Features
1. **Sales Analysis:** Visualize sales trends across time, channels, and categories.  
2. **Revenue Insights:** Track revenue by region, product, and customer demographics.  
3. **Customer Analysis:** Understand customer segmentation, repeat purchase behavior, and AOV.  
4. **Interactive Filters:** Slice by category, region, channel, and time period.  

---

## Strategic Recommendations
1. **Boost Customer Retention:** Introduce loyalty programs or personalized offers.  
2. **Optimize Sales Channels:** Focus marketing spend on top-performing platforms.  
3. **Inventory Planning:** Maintain stock for high-demand products and sizes.  
4. **Regional Expansion:** Identify underperforming regions for growth campaigns.  
5. **Promotion Planning:** Align seasonal and quarterly promotions with sales trends.
